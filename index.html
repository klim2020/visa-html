WHAT IS A VISA AND WHY YOU NEED ONE

Almost every country require foreigners to obtain a travel visa to enter their territory. Visa is a travel authorization granted by a governments to allow travelers entry for specific time and with specific purpose, for example: leisure, business, study or work. Visas can be single or multiple entry and are normally tied to a singe person. The process of obtaining the travel visas includes very specific instructions and procedures and can be complicated and time-consuming. That’s where Visagov comes into play, to help you meet all the requirements and all the deadlines. We are not part of any government departments, but we work directly with embassies to ensure that your travel plans go smoothly.
